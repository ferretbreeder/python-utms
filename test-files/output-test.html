Office of Admissions Remember that your offer of admission is contingent upon the successful completion and review of the academic information outlined in your application. We expect all admitted students to maintain their high levels of academic performance. If you believe there are any issues with the information indicated in your application, please reach out to your admission counselor. If you have been enrolled in any college courses, please also send us an official transcript from the college or university, as well as any AP test scores for evaluation. Office of Admissions
